# Case 005 Playbook

This case trains restraint. Your job is to avoid over-enforcement while still taking suspicious signals seriously.

## Step 1: Review the Alert

- Count target events and risk scores.
- Review policy signals.
- Identify why the alert fired.

## Step 2: Add Context

- Join account metadata.
- Check trust tier and organization.
- Compare prompt categories against clear abuse cases.

## Step 3: Build Both Arguments

- Why this might be abuse
- Why this might be authorized training or testing

## Step 4: Tune the Decision

Recommend no action, monitor, or detection tuning if the evidence supports benign use.
