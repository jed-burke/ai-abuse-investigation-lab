# Case 010 Playbook

This final case is intentionally less guided. Work like a senior investigator.

## Step 1: Define Scope

- What accounts and time windows are in scope?
- Which cases matter?
- What would change the decision?

## Step 2: Build Evidence Tables

Create summaries for:

- account metadata
- event volume
- average and max risk
- shared infrastructure
- policy-signal overlap
- response outcomes

## Step 3: Split the Accounts

Do not force one decision across all accounts. Decide account by account.

## Step 4: Write the Memo

Include:

- decision
- confidence
- strongest evidence
- caveats
- recommended next steps
