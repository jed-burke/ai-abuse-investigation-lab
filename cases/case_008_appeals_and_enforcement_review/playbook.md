# Case 008 Playbook

Appeals require humility. You are checking whether the original decision still holds after context is added.

## Step 1: Reconstruct the Original Concern

- Count policy signals.
- Identify highest-risk events.
- Explain why a warning may have occurred.

## Step 2: Review Context

- Join account metadata.
- Compare target risk with clearer abuse and false-positive cases.
- Look for repeat behavior or lack of recurrence.

## Step 3: Evaluate Proportionality

- Was the action too harsh, too light, or reasonable?
- Should any control be removed?
- Should the rule be tuned?

## Step 4: Write Appeal Outcome

Keep the tone factual and specific.
