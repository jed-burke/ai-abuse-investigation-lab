# Case 002 Playbook

Your job is to turn weak linkage signals into a defensible cluster assessment.

## Step 1: Identify the Cluster

- Count events by account for `case_002`.
- Review account creation dates and trust tiers.
- Find all IP addresses used by `acct_3104`.

## Step 2: Test Shared Infrastructure

- Which accounts share the target's IPs?
- Do they also share user agents?
- Are shared signals concentrated in the same time window?

## Step 3: Compare Behavior

- Count policy signals by account.
- Compare average and max risk scores.
- Check whether the same prompt categories recur across accounts.

## Step 4: Decide

Write a short cluster assessment:

- Strong links
- Weak links
- Coincidence risks
- Recommended action
