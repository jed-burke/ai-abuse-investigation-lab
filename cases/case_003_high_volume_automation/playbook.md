# Case 003 Playbook

Automation is not automatically abuse. Your job is to separate expected batch work from risky scale.

## Step 1: Measure Volume

- Count events by account.
- Find events per minute for the target.
- Compare target volume with baseline accounts.

## Step 2: Look for Repetition

- Count repeated prompt categories.
- Check whether the same policy signals recur.
- Compare risk scores over the burst.

## Step 3: Review Account Context

- Join account metadata.
- Consider trust tier and plan type.
- Check whether a new free account shares the same pattern.

## Step 4: Recommend Controls

Choose between no action, monitor, rate-limit, or escalation.
